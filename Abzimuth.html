Abzimuth

Motto: “Our spirit will endure”

Associated colors: Turquoise, titanium, and glass

Geography:The ring of land is mountainous and rocky around the outer edges and a rainforest around the inside edges with a massive expanse of water that is attached to the ocean in the center called the Muthian Sea.

Climate: Similar to Eventus, there is seasonal weather here but in a generally mild way. The only regions that see a colder winter are the very southern peaks on the outside of the ring.

Role in the World: Abzimuth exports some of the most precious materials in the world specializing in gems and stones.

Important Structures: pyramids

General: Abzimuth is fiercely tribal and will protect its own assets over everything. They have trade with the globe because it is beneficial for them to do so but they would have no qualms about locking out all outsiders without a hesitation and with general support from most of their citizens.

Abzimuth is extremely well outfitted with advanced technologies purchased with the money from their precious exports and the result is a dedication to ornate ceremony merged with the modern benefits of technology.

Culture: The Abzimi villages are often small and centrally focused coming together only to interact on the international stage and separating again to live their own lives. There is some fighting between families or tribes but generally not as the Abzimi will always prioritize another Abzimi over a foreigner. Abzimi are some of the least trusting people you will meet but they have a keen eye for a traitor for it. Befriending an Abzimi is challenging but if you can pull it off, you will have a loyal friend for the rest of your life. Abzimi can be a bit bigoted because of their fierce dedication to who they consider family and for that reason it is very difficult to see an Abzimi befriend someone who does not look like them.

At the heart of Abzimuth stands the Arbiter, a figure of significant importance in the realm's intricate balance. As a symbol of authority, wisdom, and decision-making, the Arbiter plays a vital role in shaping Abzimuth's destiny and interactions with the world beyond.
