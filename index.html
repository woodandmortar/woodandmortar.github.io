Salmon Fund was founded in April 2019 in the heart of urban Kansas City Metro. Micheal noticed a number of negative trends amidst his tradespeople, community members, and property owners. Tradespeople did not have the resources to succeed in the workplace such as regular transportation for their commutes and connectivity via smart device for both work functions and communication. Community members did not trust incoming property management which caused property damage and theft. Lastly, property owners were regularly more concerned with the bottom line and the bottom dollar over the safety and well being of the tenants residing in the properties they owned.
